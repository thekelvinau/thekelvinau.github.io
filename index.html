Abelian and realistic sandpiles

Sand pours into the centre of the grid and spills over to adjacent cells when a maximum threshold is reached in an Abelian sandpile. The spectra of sand avalanche sizes are visualized. A realistic sandpile spills into adjacent cells when a difference in the number of sand grains with adjacent cells is reached, which forms a more conical sandpile compared to the Abelian sandpile.
